Story 04 · ArXiv Research

Benchmarking Study: Autonomous Coding Agents vs. Senior Engineers on Production Incidents

Researchers at Stanford and ETH Zürich report that best-in-class coding agents now resolve 34% of real-world production incidents faster than human senior engineers, with a critical caveat: agent solutions have a 12% higher regression rate downstream.

Analysis

Speed without regression resistance isn't a win — it's a liability shift. The 12% regression figure is the real headline. Until that gap closes, "autonomous incident resolution" will remain a supervised feature, not an autonomous one, regardless of how benchmarks are framed.
