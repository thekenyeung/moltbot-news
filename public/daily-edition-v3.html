Benchmarking Autonomous Coding Agents Against Senior Engineers on Production Incidents

Stanford and ETH Zürich report that best-in-class agents resolve 34% of production incidents faster than senior engineers — with a critical caveat: agent solutions carry a 12% higher downstream regression rate.

analysis

Speed without regression resistance isn't a win — it's a liability shift. The 12% regression figure is the real headline. Until that gap closes, "autonomous incident resolution" will remain supervised, regardless of how benchmarks are framed.
